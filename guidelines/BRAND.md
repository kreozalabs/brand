# Kreoza Brand Guidelines 📐

This repository serves as the absolute source of truth for **Kreoza Labs** visual identity.

## 💎 Visual Philosophy: "Indigo Flow"
Kreoza is not a tool; it's a **Creative Partner**. Our identity should avoid "heavy" blocks and instead focus on **nodes, connections, and smooth flowing lines**.

### 🎨 Core Color Palette
- **Primary**: `Indigo Flow` (`#6366f1`) – Represents orchestration and focus.
- **Secondary**: `Deep Space` (`#0f172a`) – Our professional dark background.
- **Accent**: `Teal Pulse` (`#2dd4bf`) – For real-time updates and success indicators.

## 📐 Logo Usage
1.  **Clearance**: Always keep a "clear zone" around the logo equal to the width of the main 'K' stem.
2.  **Backgrounds**: Use the `Indigo Flow` on `Deep Space` background for a premium look.
3.  **Typography**: We use **Inter** for body text and **Outfit** for headlines.
